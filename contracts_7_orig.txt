Definition of Confidential Information: The term "Confidential Information" shall include any proprietary or confidential information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, including, but not limited to, technical data, business plans, marketing strategies, and client information.

Non-Compete Agreement: The Receiving Party agrees not to engage in any business activities that directly compete with the services offered by the Disclosing Party for a period of two years from the date of this Agreement.
