EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT
This Employment Contract ("Contract") is made and entered into on this 4th day of May 2023, by and between Sarah Lee ("Employee") and XYZ Industries, Inc. ("Employer").
Employment. Employer hereby employs Employee in the position of Human Resources Manager, and Employee hereby accepts such employment, subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this Contract.
Duties. Employee shall perform the duties and responsibilities as set forth in Exhibit A attached hereto and made a part hereof.
Compensation. Employee shall be paid a salary of $90,000 per year, payable in bi-weekly installments. In addition, Employee shall be eligible for a performance bonus of up to 15% of Employee's annual salary, subject to the achievement of certain performance objectives.
Benefits. Employee shall be entitled to participate in any employee benefit plan that Employer may offer from time to time to its employees.
Term. The term of this Contract shall commence on May 15, 2023, and shall continue until terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days written notice to the other party.
Confidentiality. Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all confidential and proprietary information of Employer, and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of Employer.
Governing Law. This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Contract as of the date first above written.
Sarah Lee
Employee
XYZ Industries, Inc.
