Employment Agreement
Global Logistics Solutions
This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into by and between Global Logistics Solutions ("Company") and Michael Reynolds ("Employee") on July 15, 2023.
Position and Responsibilities
The Company employs the Employee as a Warehouse Supervisor. The Employee will be responsible for overseeing daily warehouse operations, managing inventory, coordinating shipments, and ensuring compliance with safety regulations.
Compensation
The Employee will receive a monthly salary of $4,500, payable on the 15th day of each month. In addition, the Employee will be eligible for performance-based incentives and annual salary reviews, based on the Company's discretion.
Employment Term
This Agreement shall commence on August 1, 2023, and shall continue for an initial term of two (2) years. Upon completion of the initial term, this Agreement may be extended by mutual agreement between the Company and the Employee.
Work Schedule
The Employee's regular work schedule shall be Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The Employee may be required to work additional hours as needed to fulfill job responsibilities.
Non-Competition
During the employment term and for a period of one (1) year following the termination of this Agreement, the Employee agrees not to engage in any employment or business activities that directly compete with the Company's operations within a 50-mile radius of its headquarters.
Signed on this 15th day of July, 2023.
