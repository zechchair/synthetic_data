Date: February 5th, 2023
Time: 9:00 AM
Location: Conference Room A, 2nd Floor, Grand Plaza Hotel, Downtown Cityville
Attendees:
Emily Anderson, CEO
Daniel Fadian, COO
Olivia Wilson, CMO
Nathan Davis, CFO
Agenda:
Review of company performance for Q4 2023
Discussion on employee benefits and compensation
Approval of the annual budget for 2024
Minutes:
Emily Anderson opened the meeting by reviewing the company's performance for Q4 2023. The revenue had exceeded expectations by 25%, and the profit margins were higher than the target. All attendees expressed satisfaction with the financial results.
Daniel Fadian led the discussion on employee benefits and compensation. He presented a proposal for improving employee benefits and increasing compensation for some roles. The proposal was discussed, and all attendees agreed to the improvements.
Nathan Davis presented the annual budget for 2024, which included the cost of the employee benefit improvements and other strategic investments. The budget was discussed, and a few minor adjustments were made before approval.
Action Items:
Daniel Fadian to implement the employee benefit and compensation improvements.
Nathan Davis to finalize the annual budget for 2024 and distribute it to the board of directors.
