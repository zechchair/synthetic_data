Date: May 9, 2023
Attendees:
Andrew Park, CEO of Park Enterprises
Karen Lee, COO of Park Enterprises
James Kim, CMO of Park Enterprises
Grace Park, Head of Human Resources
Meeting Agenda: Discuss employee retention strategies
The meeting was called to order at 2:00 PM by Andrew Park, CEO of Park Enterprises. The attendees discussed employee retention strategies for the company. Grace Park, Head of Human Resources, presented a detailed plan which included employee engagement initiatives and professional development programs.
James Kim, CMO of Park Enterprises, provided feedback on the proposed plan and made suggestions for improvement. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for improvement.
Karen Lee, COO of Park Enterprises, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's budget. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed retention strategies.
The meeting was adjourned at 3:30 PM.
