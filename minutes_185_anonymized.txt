Date: June 15, 2009
Attendees:
John Miller, CEO of Miller Corporation
Lisa Winspare, COO of Miller Corporation
Michael Davis, CMO of Miller Corporation
Olivia Roberts, Head of Human Resources
Meeting Agenda: Discuss employee retention strategies
The meeting was called to order at 4:00 PM by John Miller, CEO of Miller Corporation. The attendees discussed employee retention strategies for the company. Olivia Roberts, Head of Human Resources, presented a detailed plan which included employee engagement initiatives and professional development programs.
Michael Davis, CMO of Miller Corporation, provided feedback on the proposed plan and made suggestions for improvement. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for improvement.
Lisa Winspare, COO of Miller Corporation, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's budget. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed retention strategies.
The meeting was adjourned at 5:30 PM.
