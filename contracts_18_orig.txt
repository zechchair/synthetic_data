Confidentiality Contract 2
Company Name: Smart Solutions Inc.
Employee Name: Rachel Johnson
Confidential Information: Financial Statements
Effective Date: May 4th, 2023
Term: 5 years
This confidentiality agreement (the "Agreement") is made on May 4th, 2023, between Smart Solutions Inc. (the "Company") and Rachel Johnson (the "Employee"). The Company desires to disclose to the Employee certain Confidential Information (as defined below) for the purpose of assisting the Company in financial planning. In consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:
Confidential Information. "Confidential Information" shall mean any and all financial statements, projections, reports, or any other proprietary information that is designated as confidential by the Company.
Non-Disclosure and Non-Use. The Employee agrees to hold in strict confidence and not to disclose, communicate, or use in any manner, directly or indirectly, any Confidential Information of the Company, except as required by law or authorized in writing by the Company.
Term. This Agreement shall be effective as of the date first written above and shall continue for a period of 5 years.
