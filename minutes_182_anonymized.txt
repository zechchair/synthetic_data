Date: May 24, 2023
Attendees:
Henry Wilson, CEO of Thompson Corporation
Sophia Lee, COO of Thompson Corporation
Benjamin Scott, CMO of Thompson Corporation
Olivia Chen, Head of Customer Service
Meeting Agenda: Discuss customer retention strategies
The meeting was called to order at 10:00 AM by Henry Wilson, CEO of Thompson Corporation. The attendees discussed customer retention strategies and initiatives to improve customer satisfaction. Olivia Chen, Head of Customer Service, presented a detailed plan which included customer feedback mechanisms, service improvements, and loyalty programs.
Benjamin Scott, CMO of Thompson Corporation, provided feedback on the proposed plan and made suggestions for improvement. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for optimization.
Sophia Lee, COO of Thompson Corporation, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's operations and financials. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed customer retention strategies.
The meeting was adjourned at 12:00 PM.
