Lease Contract 2:
Contract Name: Commercial Lease Agreement between Golden Plaza Inc. and StarryTech Solutions
This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into this 1st day of September 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Golden Plaza Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Michigan, with its principal place of business at 36420 Dwight Lane, Michigan, Detroit, United States ("Landlord"), and StarryTech Solutions, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Pennsylvania, with its principal place of business at 474 Cardinal Point, Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, United States ("Tenant").
Premises: Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby rents from Landlord, a commercial space located at Suite 101, Golden Plaza, 3135 Meadow Ridge Park, Alabama, Birmingham, United States (the "Premises").
Term: The initial term of this lease shall be for three years, commencing on the Effective Date and ending on the 31st day of August 2026, unless otherwise extended or terminated as provided in this Agreement.
Rent and Payment: Tenant agrees to pay a monthly rent of $4,000, payable in advance on the first day of each month, without demand, to Landlord at the address specified in this Agreement.
Maintenance and Repairs: Tenant shall be responsible for the maintenance and repairs of the interior of the Premises, while Landlord shall be responsible for the maintenance and repairs of the common areas of the building.
