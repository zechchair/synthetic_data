Meeting Agenda: Discuss IT infrastructure and cybersecurity measures
The meeting was called to order at 3:00 PM by David Lee, CEO of Lee Corporation. The attendees discussed the company's IT infrastructure and cybersecurity measures. Emily Park, Head of IT, presented a report on the company's IT infrastructure and identified potential areas for improvement.
William Chen, CIO of Lee Corporation, presented a plan to enhance the company's cybersecurity measures. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for optimization.
Jennifer Kim, COO of Lee Corporation, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's operations and financials. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed cybersecurity measures.
The meeting was adjourned at 5:00 PM.
