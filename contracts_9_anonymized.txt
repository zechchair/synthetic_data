Contract 2:
Employment Contract between David Thompson and Stellar Solutions
Date of Contract: August 12, 2023
Position: Software Developer
This employment agreement (the "Agreement") is made between David Thompson (the "Employee") and Stellar Solutions (the "Employer") on the date stated above.
The Employee agrees to work as a Software Developer for the Employer. The Employee's duties will include, but are not limited to, developing software, testing software, and troubleshooting software issues.
The Employee will receive an annual salary of $62,000, to be paid in bi-weekly installments. The Employee will be eligible for health insurance, dental insurance, and a 401(k) plan.
The Employer may terminate the Employee's employment at any time for any reason. The Employee may terminate his employment by giving the Employer two weeks' notice.
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding of the parties and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements between them.
Signed:
David Thompson (Employee)
Emma Wilson (Representative of Stellar Solutions)
