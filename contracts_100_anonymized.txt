SALES AGREEMENT

This Sales Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of the 1st day of June, 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Global Commerce, Inc. a California corporation with its principal place of business at 861 Mayfield Lane, Pennsylvania, Wilkes Barre, United States ("Seller"), and FreshBites LLC, a Colorado limited liability company with its principal place of business at 82103 Roth Crossing, Ohio, Columbus, United States	 ("Buyer").
Sale of Goods. Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase 2,000 lbs. of organic wheat, each priced at $5, for a total purchase price of $10,000 (the "Purchase Price").
Payment. Buyer shall pay the Purchase Price in full within 30 days from the date of the invoice issued by Seller.
Delivery. Seller shall deliver the Goods to Buyer at Buyer's designated location within 30 days after receipt of payment in full.
Warranty. Seller warrants that the Goods shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship at the time of delivery.
Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements, whether oral or written, relating to its subject matter.
