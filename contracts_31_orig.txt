Contract 3:
Employment Contract between David Chen and Bright Future Co.
Date of Contract: July 15, 2023
Position: Sales Representative
This employment contract (the “Agreement”) is made between David Chen (the “Employee”) and Bright Future Co. (the “Employer”) on the date stated above.
The Employee agrees to work as a Sales Representative for the Employer. The Employee’s duties will include, but are not limited to, identifying potential customers, presenting product information, and closing sales deals.
The Employee will receive an annual salary of $60,000, to be paid in bi-weekly installments. The Employer will provide the Employee with health insurance, dental insurance, and a 401(k) plan.
This Agreement may be terminated by either party with two weeks’ notice. The Employee may be terminated immediately for cause, including but not limited to, violation of company policies or failure to meet job performance expectations.
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding of the parties and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements between them.
Signed:
David Chen (Employee)
Grace Wong (Representative of Bright Future Co.)
