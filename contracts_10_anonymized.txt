This Sales Agreement ("Agreement") is made on this 4th day of July, 2025 between Brightwood Furnishings, a Vermont corporation with its principal place of business at 789 Oak St, Burlington, VT 05401 ("Seller"), and Sarah Thompson, an individual with her principal place of residence at 321 Maple St, Boston, MA 02101 ("Buyer").
Sale of Goods. Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase the following goods (the "Goods"):
One 3-seater sofa in cream fabric
Two armchairs in black faux leather
One coffee table in mahogany wood
Two table lamps in silver finish
Purchase Price. The purchase price for the Goods is $7,500, payable by the Buyer to the Seller upon delivery of the Goods.
Delivery. The Seller shall deliver the Goods to the Buyer's residence at 321 Maple St, Boston, MA 02101, within 21 days of the date of this Agreement.
Acceptance. The Buyer shall inspect the Goods upon delivery and shall have 7 days from the date of delivery to notify the Seller of any defects or non-conformance with the specifications in this Agreement. If the Buyer fails to notify the Seller within such 7-day period, the Goods shall be deemed accepted.
Warranty. The Seller warrants that the Goods shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship for a period of 2 years from the date of delivery.
Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Massachusetts.
Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties.
