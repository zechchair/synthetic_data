Lease Contract 1
Contract Name: Horizon Cottage Lease Agreement
Date: September 17, 2024
This Lease Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is made and entered into between:
Landlord: Horizon Properties LLC, a real estate company registered under the laws of New york, represented by Mr. William Anderson, residing at 817 Elmside Hill, New york, Waco, United States.
Tenant: Mr. Robert Mitchell, an individual residing at 85 Monument Plaza, New york.
Property: The Landlord hereby leases to the Tenant the vacation cottage located at 078 Washington Lane, New York, Staten Island, United States.
Lease Term: The lease term shall commence on October 1, 2024, and shall continue for a period of three months, ending on December 31, 2024.
Rent: The Tenant shall pay a monthly rent of $2,300 (USD), payable on the first day of each month. The Tenant shall also be responsible for paying any utility bills incurred during the lease term.
Security Deposit: The Tenant shall provide a security deposit of $1,800 (USD) prior to the commencement of the lease term. The security deposit shall be returned to the Tenant within 30 days after the end of the lease term, subject to deductions for any damages or outstanding rent.
Use of Property: The Tenant shall use the property solely for residential purposes and shall not engage in any illegal or disruptive activities that may disturb the peace and quiet of the neighborhood.
Termination: Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice to the other party at least 30 days prior to the intended termination date.
