Confidentiality Contract 1:
Contract Name: Non-Disclosure Agreement between Golden Shield Inc. and SecureGuard Innovations
This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into this 27th day of September 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Golden Shield Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Delaware, with its principal place of business at 987 Elm Street, Anycity, USA ("Disclosing Party"), and SecureGuard Innovations, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of California, with its principal place of business at 654 Pine Avenue, Somewhereville, USA ("Receiving Party").
Confidential Information: The Disclosing Party may provide the Receiving Party with certain confidential and proprietary information ("Confidential Information") related to the business operations, products, and services. The Receiving Party agrees to keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential and to use it solely for the purposes defined in this Agreement.
Non-Disclosure: The Receiving Party agrees not to disclose, directly or indirectly, any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, except as required by law.
Term and Termination: This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of two (2) years from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier by either party upon written notice. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive the termination of this Agreement.
