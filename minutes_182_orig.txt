Meeting Agenda: Discuss customer retention strategies
The meeting was called to order at 10:00 AM by William Lee, CEO of Lee Enterprises. The attendees discussed customer retention strategies and initiatives to improve customer satisfaction. Kelly Wang, Head of Customer Service, presented a detailed plan which included customer feedback mechanisms, service improvements, and loyalty programs.
Michael Park, CMO of Lee Enterprises, provided feedback on the proposed plan and made suggestions for improvement. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for optimization.
Julia Kim, COO of Lee Enterprises, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's operations and financials. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed customer retention strategies.
The meeting was adjourned at 12:00 PM.
