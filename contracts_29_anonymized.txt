This Sales Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of the 1st day of June, 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between VisionCorp, Inc. a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Elm St., Springfield, NY 12345 ("Seller"), and GreenWave LLC, a Nevada limited liability company with its principal place of business at 456 Oak Street, Cityville, NV 67890 ("Buyer").
Sale of Goods. Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase 500 units of the Gadget model Z, each priced at $2,000, for a total purchase price of $1,000,000 (the "Purchase Price").
