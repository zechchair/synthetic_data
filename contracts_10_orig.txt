This Sales Agreement ("Agreement") is made on this 4th day of May, 2023 between Urban Interiors, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Main St, Wilmington, DE 19801 ("Seller"), and Mary Johnson, an individual with her principal place of residence at 456 Elm St, New York, NY 10001 ("Buyer").
Sale of Goods. Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase the following goods (the "Goods"):
One 3-seater sofa in beige fabric
Two armchairs in grey leather
One coffee table in oak wood
Two table lamps in bronze finish
Purchase Price. The purchase price for the Goods is $5,000, payable by the Buyer to the Seller upon delivery of the Goods.
Delivery. The Seller shall deliver the Goods to the Buyer's residence at 456 Elm St, New York, NY 10001, within 14 days of the date of this Agreement.
Acceptance. The Buyer shall inspect the Goods upon delivery and shall have 5 days from the date of delivery to notify the Seller of any defects or non-conformance with the specifications in this Agreement. If the Buyer fails to notify the Seller within such 5-day period, the Goods shall be deemed accepted.
Warranty. The Seller warrants that the Goods shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship for a period of 1 year from the date of delivery.
Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.
Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties.
