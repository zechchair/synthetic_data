Meeting Agenda: Discuss IT infrastructure and cybersecurity measures
The meeting was called to order at 2:00 PM by Daniel Evans, CEO of Evans Corporation. The attendees discussed the company's IT infrastructure and cybersecurity measures. Olivia Mitchell, Head of IT, presented a report on the company's IT infrastructure and identified potential areas for improvement.
Michael Taylor, CIO of Evans Corporation, presented a plan to enhance the company's cybersecurity measures. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for optimization.
Jessica Nguyen, COO of Evans Corporation, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's operations and financials. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed cybersecurity measures.
The meeting was adjourned at 4:00 PM.
