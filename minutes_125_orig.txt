Meeting Agenda: Discuss marketing campaign for new product launch
The meeting was called to order at 10:00 AM by Michael McLesee, CEO ofMcLesese Corporation. The attendees discussed the marketing campaign for the company's new product launch. Sarah Lee, Head of Marketing, presented a detailed plan which included social media advertising and influencer partnerships.
Albrecht Chaplainain, CFO of McLesee Corporation, provided feedback on the proposed budget and made suggestions for cost-saving measures. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the proposed plan and made additional suggestions for improvement.
Emily Davis, COO of McLesee Corporation, raised concerns about the potential impact on the company's operations. The attendees discussed the potential benefits and drawbacks of the proposed marketing campaign.
The meeting was adjourned at 11:30 AM.
