Meeting Agenda: Discuss new product development
The meeting was called to order at 2:00 PM by Andrew Grocutt, CEO of Grocutt Corp. The attendees discussed the development of a new product. Daniel Kim, Head of Product Development, presented a prototype of the product and the attendees provided feedback on the design and functionality.
Matthew Wong, CTO of Grocutt Corp, raised concerns about the technical feasibility of the product. The attendees discussed potential solutions and agreed to review the technical specifications.
Sophia Chen, COO of Grocutt Corp, presented a timeline for the product development and launch. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the timeline and made suggestions for improvement.
