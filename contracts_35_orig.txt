Confidentiality Contract 1:
Contract Name: Non-Disclosure Agreement between Global Solutions Inc. and SecureTech Innovations
This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into this 15th day of May 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Global Solutions Inc., a corporation organized and existing under the laws of Ohio, with its principal place of business at 82103 Roth Crossing, Ohio, Columbus, United States ("Disclosing Party"), and SecureTech Innovations, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of California, with its principal place of business at 6662 Armistice Junction, Louisiana, Lafayette, United States ("Receiving Party").
Confidential Information: The Disclosing Party may provide the Receiving Party with certain confidential and proprietary information ("Confidential Information") related to the business operations, products, and services. The Receiving Party agrees to keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential and to use it solely for the purposes defined in this Agreement.
Non-Disclosure: The Receiving Party agrees not to disclose, directly or indirectly, any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party, except as required by law.
Term and Termination: This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of three (3) years from the Effective Date, unless terminated earlier by either party upon written notice. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive the termination of this Agreement.
