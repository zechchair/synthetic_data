Lease Contract 1
Contract Name: Lakeside Cottage Lease Agreement
Date: May 9, 2023
This Lease Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is made and entered into between:
Landlord: Lakeside Properties LLC, a real estate company registered under the laws of Yangdang, represented by Mr. Benjamin Reynolds, residing at 13 Shasta Crossing, Yangdang, China.
Tenant: Mr. David Shurville, an individual residing at 49 Straubel Center, Jiangnan, China.
Property: The Landlord hereby leases to the Tenant the vacation cottage located at 4 Swallow Park, Hexing, China.
Lease Term: The lease term shall commence on June 1, 2023, and shall continue for a period of three months, ending on August 31, 2023.
Rent: The Tenant shall pay a monthly rent of $2,000 (USD), payable on the first day of each month. The Tenant shall also be responsible for paying any utility bills incurred during the lease term.
Security Deposit: The Tenant shall provide a security deposit of $1,500 (USD) prior to the commencement of the lease term. The security deposit shall be returned to the Tenant within 30 days after the end of the lease term, subject to deductions for any damages or outstanding rent.
Use of Property: The Tenant shall use the property solely for residential purposes and shall not engage in any illegal or disruptive activities that may disturb the peace and quiet of the neighborhood.
Termination: Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice to the other party at least 30 days prior to the intended termination date.
