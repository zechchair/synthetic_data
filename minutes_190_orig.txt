Attendees:
James Lee, CEO of Lee Enterprises

Meeting Agenda: Discuss new product development
The meeting was called to order at 2:00 PM by James Lee, CEO of Lee Enterprises. The attendees discussed the development of a new product. Steven Kim, Head of Product Development, presented a prototype of the product and the attendees provided feedback on the design and functionality.
Michael Wong, CTO of Lee Enterprises, raised concerns about the technical feasibility of the product. The attendees discussed potential solutions and agreed to review the technical specifications.
Lily Chen, COO of Lee Enterprises, presented a timeline for the product development and launch. The attendees discussed the feasibility of the timeline and made suggestions for improvement.
The meeting was adjourned at 3:30 PM.
